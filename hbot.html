WHAT IS HBOT?

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) is a non-invasive treatment that delivers pure oxygen in a pressurized chamber to boost the body’s natural healing processes. By increasing the amount of oxygen your blood can carry, HBOT enhances cell repair, reduces inflammation, supports brain function, and promotes faster recovery — from everyday fatigue to serious injuries. It’s wellness, recharged at the cellular level.
